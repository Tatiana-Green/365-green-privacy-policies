Introduction

Welcome to Xplain Language! We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we handle your information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

Email Address (Optional)

  • When you subscribe to premium features, we collect your email address for account management and subscription verification
  • Used only for authentication and subscription management
  • Not shared with third parties

Text Input

  • The words, phrases, or sentences you enter for explanation
  • Sent to OpenAI API for processing
  • Not stored permanently on our servers
  • Cached temporarily in your device's local storage for history feature

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

Usage Data

  • App usage statistics (number of explanations requested per day)
  • Device type and operating system version
  • App version
  • Language preference
  • Used to enforce daily free usage limits (10 explanations/day for free users)

Analytics

  • We use Firebase Analytics to understand app usage patterns
  • Data is anonymized and aggregated
  • Helps us improve the app experience

1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

We do NOT collect:

  • Your name or personal identity information
  • Location data
  • Contact lists
  • Photos or media files
  • Payment information (handled securely by Google Play Store)

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Core Functionality

AI Explanations

  • Your text input is sent to OpenAI's API to generate language learning explanations
  • OpenAI processes this data according to their privacy policy
  • We do not store your explanations on our servers

Usage Limits

  • We track daily usage to enforce the 10 free explanations per day limit
  • Premium subscribers have unlimited access

2.2 App Improvement

  • Anonymous usage statistics help us understand which features are most valuable
  • Crash reports help us fix bugs and improve stability
  • Analytics data is aggregated and cannot identify individual users

2.3 Subscription Management

  • Email addresses are used solely for subscription verification
  • We may send essential account-related emails (e.g., subscription confirmations)
  • We will never send marketing emails without your consent

3. Data Sharing and Third Parties

3.1 Third-Party Services

OpenAI API

  • Your text input is sent to OpenAI for processing
  • OpenAI's privacy policy: https://openai.com/privacy
  • Data is transmitted securely via HTTPS

Firebase (Google)

  • Analytics and Remote Configuration
  • Privacy policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

Google Play Store

  • Subscription payments are processed by Google Play
  • We do not have access to your payment information
  • Google's privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

3.2 No Data Sales

We do NOT sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

Explanation History

  • Stored locally on your device using Hive (local database)
  • Not synchronized to cloud servers
  • Deleted when you uninstall the app

Preferences

  • Language preference and settings stored locally
  • Not accessible to us or third parties

4.2 Server-Side Data

Minimal Server Storage

  • We do NOT maintain a central database of user explanations
  • API keys are stored securely in Firebase Remote Config (not accessible to users)
  • Email addresses for premium subscribers are stored securely

4.3 Security Measures

  • All API communications use HTTPS encryption
  • Email addresses are stored in secure Firebase Authentication
  • We follow industry-standard security practices
